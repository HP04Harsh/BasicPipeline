About Gondia

Gondia is a peaceful city in eastern Maharashtra known for its rice production, lush greenery, and proximity to wildlife sanctuaries. Often called the “Rice City”, it serves as an important agricultural and railway hub connecting Maharashtra, Chhattisgarh, and Madhya Pradesh.

Top Attractions

Navegaon National Park

A beautiful forest reserve famous for wildlife, bird watching, and a scenic lake.

Navegaon Dam

A popular tourist spot with breathtaking sunset views and peaceful nature surroundings.

Hazra Falls

A hidden natural waterfall perfect for nature lovers and monsoon trips.

Kachargadh Caves

A sacred and historical cave site associated with tribal culture and mythology.

Why Visit Gondia?

Beautiful forests, wildlife reserves, peaceful environment, and rich tribal culture make Gondia a hidden gem in central India.

Culture & Lifestyle

Tribal Heritage

The region has a strong Gond tribal culture with unique traditions and festivals.

Rice Capital

Gondia is widely known for rice mills and agricultural production.

Local Festivals

Festivals like Diwali, Holi, and traditional tribal celebrations bring vibrant community life.

Travel Information

By Train

Gondia Junction is a major railway station connecting major cities.

By Air

The nearest airport is Nagpur Airport, about 150 km away.

Best Time to Visit

October to February offers pleasant weather for travel.
